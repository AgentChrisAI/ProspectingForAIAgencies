# Top 10 Product Demo Priority List

## Purpose
This document defines the first ten Sunburnt AI products that should receive structured demo treatment inside the Sunburnt Sales App.

These products were selected using four criteria:
1. commercial usefulness in live calls
2. ease of visual demonstration
3. relevance across multiple industries
4. likelihood of closing or advancing deals when shown well

---

# Prioritization Logic

The first demo set should not simply be the ten most technically interesting products.
It should be the ten most commercially effective products to demonstrate with low friction.

The strongest early demo candidates typically share these characteristics:
- obvious before/after transformation
- easy-to-understand inputs and outputs
- broad relevance across industries
- can be shown without deep integration complexity
- map directly to strong pain points

---

# Final Recommendation

Do not try to demo all products equally from day one.

Focus the first demo investment on these ten products because they give the best combination of:
- commercial usefulness
- demo simplicity
- visual clarity
- cross-industry relevance
- close-driving potential

Once these ten are strong, expand the library systematically.
